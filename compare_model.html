v10's reward had a critical imbalance: missile interception gave 70x more reward than killing enemies, causing the AI to hunt missiles while ignoring descending enemies. v11 fixed this by adding enemy/monster kill bonuses, reducing missile bonus, and adding a progressive descent penalty. v12 further scaled the descent penalty with level (2.5x at level 8+) and added mixed-start curriculum for high-level exposure.

★ Verdict ★

PPO v12 is the new champion with a 185,460 best score, 141K best average, and avg level 7.3 — surpassing v10's records across the board. The key breakthroughs were reward rebalancing (v11) and level-scaled descent penalty + mixed-start curriculum (v12).

v10's fatal flaw: missile interception was 70x more rewarding than killing enemies, causing the AI to hunt missiles while enemies descended to the wall. Games ended with 5+ lives remaining but enemies at the wall — instant game over regardless of lives.

v11 fixed rewards: added enemy kill bonus (+1.0), monster kill bonus (+3.0), reduced missile bonus (2.0→0.5), reduced life-loss penalty (5.0→3.0), and added progressive descent penalty. Result: avg level 6.5, zero-hit games through level 7.

v12 added curriculum: level-scaled descent penalty (2.5x stronger at level 8+) and 25% of training episodes starting at levels 4-7 for high-level exposure. The model now routinely plays level 8 with zero hits and reaches level 9 with 4-5 lives remaining.
