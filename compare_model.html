★ Verdict ★

The eyeBMinvaders v10 model solves a fundamentally harder problem than any published Atari SpaceInvaders benchmark. Standard models from HuggingFace (sb3 DQN, community submissions) are vanilla DQNs trained on the simple Atari 2600 ROM — they cannot play this game, let alone compete. Even state-of-the-art algorithms like Rainbow, Agent57, and MuZero are designed for the original Atari environment with no kamikazes, no homing missiles, no bosses, and no multi-level progression. Consistently reaching Level 9 with a 127K average score demonstrates a sophisticated learned policy that handles simultaneous multi-type threats, dynamic difficulty scaling, and strategic resource management — capabilities none of the published benchmarks have ever been tested against.
